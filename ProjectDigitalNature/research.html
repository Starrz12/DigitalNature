Current Areas of Research

Funding for research is driven by two main areas.

  • Government funding
  • Private Businesses

Current research is mainly focused in areas seen to better human quality of life or as good business investments.
Significant resources are being poured into:

  • Health Sciences
  • Robotics
  • Automation
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Alternative Energy
  • Food Sourcing
  • Safety
  • Better Understanding our World
  • Better Understanding Human Behavior

Research is welcome in all areas! The more 'off track' from main avenues of research a subject is the more difficult it will be to find funding for the project.
